Event Details IEDC summit 2022

KSUM is organizing the sixth edition of the flagship event IEDC Summit 2022, bringing together Student Innovators and all other connected stakeholders under one umbrella. This year, the IEDC Summit is hosted by St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai on 15th January 2022

IEDC Summit 2022, the annual get together is hosted by Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) of St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai. The Summit will witness a series of technology tracks, brainstorming sessions, interactive community meet ups and product exhibitions. Student innovators from IEDCs exhibit their innovation at IEDC Summit. Besides, knowledge and skill development sessions are an integral part of the summit and the presence of corporate leaders provides students a deeper insight about latest technology trends.

ABOUT

IEDC Summit 2022

Government of Kerala initiated the Startup movement through Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) by forging and implementing forward-looking policies for creating a vibrant Startup ecosystem in the state primarily to foster the growth of innovation lead technology entrepreneurship by supporting all the strata of society to encourage them to come up with ideas and to turn it into business so that they may obtain gainful employment and can employ others as well.

KSUM organizes the sixth edition of the flagship event IEDC Summit 2022, bringing together Student Innovators and all other connected stakeholders under one umbrella. This year, the IEDC Summit is hosted by St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology, Palai 15th January 2022

IEDC Summit 2022, the annual get together is hosted by Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) of St. Joseph's College of Engineering and Technology. The Summit will witness a series of technology tracks, brainstorming sessions, interactive community meet ups and product exhibitions. Student innovators from IEDCs exhibit their innovation at IEDC Summit. Besides, knowledge and skill development sessions are an integral part of the summit and the presence of corporate leaders provides students a deeper insight about latest technology trends.

The summit is designed for young generation entrepreneurs in two major phases - The main event and the Exhibition Stall


THE CONTEXT

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centres (IEDC) are platforms set up mainly in Engineering, Arts & Science Colleges, Management Institutions, Polytechnics etc with an aim to provide students an opportunity to innovate and experiment. The outbreak of COVID-19 has not only created a health crisis but also threatened our ways of living and the disruption brought in the Academic sphere has no parallel in history.

But we had Inspire to Innovate webinars, Innovation at the time of Corona product showcase and Innovations Unlocked: Mentorship modules with Industry experts and other various programmes and events initiated by the Nodal Officers to engage and empower the Student innovators activity thereby propelled their creativity to something wonderful and remarkable.

IEDC Summit is also an avenue for participants of various centers across the State to come together, to share their success stories, to learn from each other’s failures, to showcase their innovative products and also to develop strong networks that go a long way in developing sustainable enterprises.

Kerala Startup Mission has set up IEDCs in 281 institutions across the State who are having requisite expertise and infrastructure which provides avenues for creative students to learn, collaborate and transform their innovative ideas into prototypes of viable products.
